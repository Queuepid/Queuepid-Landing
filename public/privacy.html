Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Queuepid (“we”, “us”) operates the queuepid.gg landing page and waitlist. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what your choices are. It applies to the waitlist only — when the Queuepid product launches, it will have its own privacy policy covering the service itself.

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you choose to join the waitlist.
  • IP address — collected at the moment you submit the waitlist form. We use it to derive an approximate location (city, region, country) and ISP via ip-api.com, then include it in an internal signup notification. We do not store it ourselves beyond what our hosting provider logs.
  • Standard server logs — our hosting provider (Vercel) automatically logs technical data such as IP address and browser type for security and operations, retained briefly.

This site uses no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking.

How we use your email

  • To send a one-time confirmation that you joined the waitlist.
  • To let you know when Queuepid launches and share occasional pre-launch updates.

That's it. We will never sell your email address or share it with third parties for their own marketing. Our legal basis for this processing is your consent, which you give by submitting the form and may withdraw at any time.

Who processes your data

  • Resend (resend.com) — stores the waitlist and delivers our emails.
  • Vercel (vercel.com) — hosts this site.
  • ip-api.com — receives your IP address at signup to return approximate geolocation data (city, region, country, ISP). Their privacy policy is available at ip-api.com.

Each acts as a processor on our behalf. Their infrastructure may be located in the United States.

How long we keep it

We keep your email until the waitlist program ends, you unsubscribe, or you ask us to delete it — whichever comes first.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to access, correct, or delete your data, or to withdraw consent. Email hello@queuepid.gg and we'll handle it promptly. Every marketing email we send will also include an unsubscribe link. Depending on where you live (e.g., the EU/EEA, UK, or California), you may have additional rights under local law, including the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

Children

The waitlist is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update this page and the date above.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at hello@queuepid.gg.
